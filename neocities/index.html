    Welcome to my little corner of the internet!

    After spending much of my time on the system site, I realized that I was ready to have my own, because I kept trying to add too much shit to the other one. I might end up moving a lot of that stuff over to this site... but until then, I hope you like what I'm cooking up here!

    I'm a self-taught web designer who's going to the Apple Developer Academy to learn Swift. I like to think of making websites as an artistic outlet for myself, so please do see all of my projects as an extension of me.

    Use the phone to navigate and interact with the site! It will be found in the bottom right corner of every page! There's a lot going on in there, so feel free to experiment! You never know what you might find. As always though, this site is WIP, so you might find more

    If you don't need freezeframe.js, please navigate to the settings app and use the toggle to unfreeze them! I hope you enjoy exploring and make sure to come back later for more interactive pieces of the site.

    Oh, and please feel free to leave me a message on IdolMessenger! It serves as a guest book for this site.
