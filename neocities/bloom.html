    Welcome to my little corner of the internet!

    After spending much of my time on the system site, I realized that I was ready to have my own, because I kept trying to add too much shit to the other one. I might end up moving a lot of that stuff over to this site... but until then, I hope you like what I'm cooking up here!

    I'm a self-taught web designer who's going to the Apple Developer Academy to learn Swift. I like to think of making websites as an artistic outlet for myself, so please do see all of my projects as an extension of me.

    Some Tips:

    This site is currently NOT mobile compatible! I will fix that later but for now, it's designed with laptops and computers in mind!

    Use the phone to navigate and interact with the site! It will be found in the bottom right corner of every page! There's a lot going on in there, so feel free to experiment! You never know what you might find. As always though, this site is WIP, so you might find more later on!

    Almost every update to this site will break the phone. If this happens, right click on the phone screen and navigate to "open frame in new tab" (or something similar... I use Firefox!) Once you arrive at the homepage, hard refresh with ctrl + R. That usually works, but if it doesn't, you might have to wait or clear your browser's cache. Be aware that this will log you out of neocities and Chattable if you use it.

    There are music options if you'd like them. Navigate to the Serenade app to see your options! Music will not autoplay until you open the app! That choice is yours to make!

    If you don't need freezeframe.js, please navigate to the settings app and use the toggle to unfreeze them!

    I hope you enjoy exploring and make sure to come back later for more interactive pieces of the site.

    Oh, and please feel free to leave me a message on IdolMessenger! It serves as the guest book for this site.
